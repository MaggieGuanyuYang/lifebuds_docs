Privacy Policy

Life Buds Chrome Extension

Summary: Life Buds respects your privacy. We store your preferences locally on your device and only send minimal data to generate personalized affirmations. We do not sell or share your personal information.

Information We Collect

Life Buds collects and stores the following information to provide you with personalized affirmations:

  • Preferences: Your selected affirmation categories (wealth, health, relationship, general)
  • Custom Goals: Any personal goals you choose to enter for more personalized affirmations
  • Settings: Notification timing preferences, display settings, and UI preferences
  • API Keys: If you choose to use your own OpenAI or Gemini API key, it is stored locally on your device

How We Store Your Data

Your data is stored using Chrome's built-in storage system:

  • Chrome Sync Storage: Preferences and settings are synced across your Chrome browsers when signed into Chrome
  • Chrome Local Storage: Sensitive data like custom API keys and UI positions are stored only on your local device

We do not maintain external databases of your personal information.

Data We Send to Servers

When generating personalized affirmations, the following data may be sent to our API server:

  • Your selected affirmation categories
  • Your custom goals (if provided)

This data is used solely to generate relevant affirmations and is not stored on our servers.

Third-Party Services

Life Buds uses the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI API: To generate personalized affirmations using AI. When using our default service, requests are processed through our server. If you provide your own API key, requests go directly to OpenAI.
  • Google Gemini API: An alternative AI provider if you choose to use your own Gemini API key.

Please refer to OpenAI's Privacy Policy and Google's Privacy Policy for information on how they handle data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. Your data is only used to provide the core functionality of the extension.

Data Retention

Your settings and preferences are retained in Chrome storage until you:

  • Uninstall the extension
  • Clear your browser data
  • Manually reset settings within the extension

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all your stored data in the extension settings
  • Modify: Change your preferences at any time
  • Delete: Remove all data by uninstalling the extension or clearing browser data
  • Portability: Your settings sync with your Chrome account and move with you

Children's Privacy

Life Buds is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
